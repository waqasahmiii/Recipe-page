Clasic tomato pasta

Serves 4:30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 400g pasta
  • 4 Large tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and papper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil, saute garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomatoes, cook until soft.
  4. Toss pasta with sauce, season, and serve.

Nutrition(per serving)

Calories 340 kcal
Protein 11g
Carbs 58g
